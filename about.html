WELCOME TO B MAJOR MUSIC, MALVERN

B Major Music, Malvern. Is a new family ran and operated school that was established in 2025. We might be the new kids on the block but don't let that deter you, we have over 20 years of playing/production experience and over 10 years of performance experience. With a proven history of teaching and results that match.

Info@bmajormusic.com.au

WHY DO WE TEACH?

Aaron Davis is the head teacher here at B Major Music, Malvern. We had noticed that the education of music can be very easily overlooked some teachers only focus entirely on theory or entirely on practical. Here we encapsulate the student in the whole experience of music, from theory, playing, recording to artist development.

HOW WE TEACH

Aaron Davis, believes that people learn more effectively through nurturing and understanding more then a mechanical approach. each student has a unique mind and learning style. We aim to cater for each personality type and learning type. Each student will have unique homework and course structure. Sometimes music takes time so patience is required. We are here to make this fun and palettable .

Studio Branch
Ukulele Lesson
Ukulele Lesson

Learning Ukulele is a fantastic start to any person interest in music, The Instrument carries over from Culture to Culture it looks similar to Guitar, plays similar to a Guitar. But has its own little unique nuances which we love to teach.

Guitar Lesson
Guitar Lesson

Every person learns differently we understand that and cater every lesson to the student's individual learning style. We believe nurturing a student will get more results then brute force.

Piano Lesson
Piano Lesson

Piano is very similar to Guitar, it is very famous all the world over and nearly every famous composer can play it effectively. Why? Because the piano is a two handed version of Guitar. Where all the notes are laid out in front of you. Which is why it is a favourite for students to start on. It not only helps with music but with math and problem solving skills, also its just fun to play.

Vocal Lesson
Vocal Lesson

Learning to sing is one of the most daunting experiences a person can go through, your voice is your instrument and that is hard. But it is 100% unique to you that's the beauty of singing, you and the next person are totally different. Which is what makes it such a rewarding experience the fear turns into confidence which turns into eagerness. You just need to take that step!

Music Production
Music Production

Yeah, you read that right at B Major Music, we intend on covering all bases to make you the best you can be in the industry. Music Production is something that is on the rise especially in the technology age gone are days of tape and records and in come computers. Did you know you can produce music at home without the need of a producer, mixing engineer or recording studio? We will make all of those.

Music Theory Lesson
Music Theory Lesson

You might be a student studying to play the piano your abilities might not be a problem, you might have chops, skills, talent but feel you cant use it to the best of your abilities. We teach intensive Theory courses, where we just focus entirely on your knowledge base and how to use it, need to learn to read? Come on in, need to know how to build chords? Come on in. Need to learn to how to use counterpoint, come on in! Haven't learnt song structure? Come on in! We are dedicated to making you the best you can be in the industry or for yourself.
