GUITAR LESSON

PERSONALIZED LESSONS

Every person learns differently we understand that and cater every lesson to the student's individual learning style. We believe nurturing a student will get more results then brute force.

WHY CHOSE GUITAR?

Guitar is a fantastic starting point to learning music, the instrument is one of the most famous and the most accessible some people even have them lying around the house as art. Why not pick it up and come on in and learn to make it sound the way it. Don't have one? That's fine we have spares.

FLEXIBLE LESSON TIMES

At B Major Music, We understand that time is something we battle against and can make it hard to have the same time every week, which is entirely fine. We are able to move the lessons around to fit your unique schedule (24 hours notice is required for a change of time or a charge may apply)

AGE

Age is not a problem for us, we teach people over 60 and younger then 10. We will find the best way to teach you or your child and make sure you get the most out of your lessons.

NDIS PARTICIPANTS

We understand some people or children experience difficulties and are NDIS funded, we have a number of students on NDIS and we believe everyone is worth teaching and giving time to. Whether you are self funded or not we will figure out a way to get you booked in.
